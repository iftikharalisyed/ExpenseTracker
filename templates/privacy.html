1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect only the information necessary to provide the Service. This includes:

  • Account information — your name and email address provided during registration
  • Expense records — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter
  • Usage data — pages visited and actions taken within the app, used solely to improve the Service

We do not collect payment information, government-issued IDs, or any sensitive personal data beyond what is listed above.

2. How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is used exclusively to operate and improve Spendly. Specifically:

  • To authenticate you and provide access to your account
  • To display and organise your expense records
  • To generate category summaries and spending insights for your personal use
  • To respond to support requests you initiate
  • To diagnose technical issues and improve the reliability of the Service

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond the above. We will never sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored in a secured database. Passwords are never stored in plain text — they are hashed using industry-standard algorithms (Werkzeug's generate_password_hash) before being saved.

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your expense records and personal information will be permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.

While we take reasonable measures to protect your data, no system is completely secure. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your Spendly account.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly uses a small number of third-party services to deliver its user interface:

  • Google Fonts — fonts are loaded from Google's servers. Google may collect your IP address as part of this request. See Google's Privacy Policy for details.

Beyond the above, we do not integrate with any external analytics, advertising, or data-sharing services. Your expense data never leaves our own infrastructure.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Export your expense records at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details below. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, the "Last updated" date at the top of this page will be revised. Continued use of the Service after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Questions about your privacy or this policy? Contact us at privacy@spendly.com and we'll get back to you promptly.
